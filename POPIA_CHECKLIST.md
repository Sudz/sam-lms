# POPIA Readiness Checklist (SAM-LMS)

1. Data inventory
- Identify personal data collected (learners, teachers, billing info).
- Map storage locations and retention periods.

2. Lawful basis & consent
- Document lawful basis for each data processing activity.
- Implement consent capture where needed (registration, marketing).

3. Privacy notices
- Publish privacy policy in English and Afrikaans; provide both simple and detailed versions.

4. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
- Draft a DPA template for customers and partners.

5. Data residency & transfers
- Host production data in South Africa.
- Document third-party processors and any cross-border transfers.

6. Security controls
- Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.
- RBAC, audit logs, and periodic penetration testing.

7. Breach response
- Incident response plan and notification process aligned with local expectations.

8. Rights of data subjects
- Implement data access, rectification, deletion flows.
- Provide exportable user data in a machine-readable format.

9. Staff & training
- Privacy training for staff who handle PII.

10. Record keeping
- Maintain processing activity logs and consent records.
