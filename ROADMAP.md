# SAM-LMS Roadmap (public) — focused on South Africa (2026)

Summary
- Objective: Become the default LMS for South African small-to-medium education and training providers by the end of 2026.
- Themes: Trust & Compliance, Local Integrations, Mobile & Offline, Channel Partnerships, Curriculum Fit.

0–3 months (Quick wins)
- POPIA checklist + privacy policy draft.
- ZAR invoice template + billing in ZAR.
- Integrate one local payment provider (Ozow/PayFast) + one SMS provider (Infobip/Clickatell).
- Docker + GitHub Action demo deploy.

3–6 months
- PWA offline sync and seed mobile app.
- Basic i18n (English, Afrikaans, isiZulu).
- Launch 3 pilot programmes (school, corporate L&D, training provider).
- Reseller programme documentation.

6–12 months
- Enterprise features: SSO (SAML/OIDC), RBAC, audit logs, backups in SA.
- Certificate issuance & SETA reporting templates.
- Sign 3+ channel partners (telco / reseller / fintech).

12–24 months
- Scale pilots into paid customers, optimise pricing, and run national marketing campaigns.
- Deep telco / fintech integration and co-marketing.
- Expand curriculum templates for provincial/regional needs.

KPIs
- Businesses onboarded, activation rate, MRR (ZAR), churn, DAU/MAU, partner sign-ups.
